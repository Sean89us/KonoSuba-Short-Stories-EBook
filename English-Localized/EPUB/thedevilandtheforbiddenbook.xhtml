The Devil and the Forbidden Book

Volume 7 Toranora special short story

Translator: Cannongerbil

Editors: Ulti

Localization: Sean92us (Utilizing GPT-5.2)

Occurrence: Volume 7

This happened one night when I was out late and heading home.

“…What’s going on?”

Even though it was this late, for some reason there was a huge line tucked beside an alley.

And I recognized a few faces in it.

Curiosity got the better of me, so I walked over and asked,

“Uh, excuse me—what’s this line for?”

“R-Regular! What are you doing out this late?”

Standing in line were the girls from the shop—the ones who usually take care of me.

“A-A human! Why’s there a human here at this hour?”

“Oh no, we’ve been discovered! Are they canceling tonight’s sale?”

The people in line started making a fuss the second they saw me.

“N-No, it’s fine. He’s a regular at our shop. He already knows we’re succubi.”

At that, the line collectively let out a sigh of relief.

Which meant nobody in this line was human.

“Well, tonight the shop we’re lined up for is releasing an extremely shocking pinup magazine.”

An “extremely shocking” pinup magazine?

“Ever since they announced it, I’ve been counting the days! I’ve been waiting for this!”

“I-I’ve been praying to the dark gods every day since they announced it…”

That’s what the two guys at the front of the line were saying.

Once I paid attention, it was obvious: almost every devil in line looked ridiculously excited.

Was it really that intense?

“So… Regular, could you do us a favor and keep what you saw here to yourself?”

She bowed deeply as she said it.

“Of course. And actually…I’m getting in line too.”

“Huh?”

Every devil present gasped like I’d just announced my own execution.

“I-Is that really okay? I don’t know if I should be the one to say this, but it’s a really shocking magazine. If a young devil who hasn’t built up any resistance reads it… it might cost them a few of their lives.”

“Now I want to see it even more.”

“W-Who is that human? Does he have a death wish?”

“You don’t know? He’s a regular at our shop, and he’s on friendly terms with Vanir.”

Murmurs spread through the crowd. For some reason, a few of them were even looking at me like they were jealous.

Just then—

“Thank you for lining up at my shop this late, everyone! Please, come in!”

The one who announced that was Vanir.

Well, given where we were standing, I’d had a pretty good guess…

The devils rushed the counter, and anyone who managed to get a copy held it like it was priceless treasure.

“Hey, hurry up and turn the page! I want to see what’s next!”

“I-I get it, but calm down! D-Don’t let it get to your head! This page is way too stimulating…”

The male devils at the front immediately buried themselves in the magazine, like they couldn’t hold back for even one second.

“Kyaa!”

“Oh! That’s why I said you’re still too young for this. There goes one of your lives.”

Maybe she got too worked up after hearing the commotion up front, because the young-looking succubus Aqua once caught sprayed blood from her nose and collapsed.

Was it really that amazing?

Just when my hopes were sky-high—

“Oh, my apologies—they are all sold out. Anyone still in line, please hold onto your tickets and await the next printing.”

Vanir delivered the most crushing news imaginable.

I shoved my way past the devils trudging off with gloomy expressions and marched right up to Vanir.

“Hey, hold it right there! You got my expectations up and then pull this on me!? Isn’t that a little much!?”

“Oh? I was wondering why I was tasting such delicious dark emotions. So it’s because of this pervy brat who’s head over heels for succubi. What are you doing here?”

“I’m very interested in that ‘extremely shocking’ magazine. Is there really not a single copy left?”

“You aren’t a devil, so why are you interested in it? Still, you’re an important customer. I do keep a spare copy on hand in case something goes wrong, but…”

Hearing that, the succubi I’d been talking to earlier started making a fuss.

“H-How could you!? Regular, that’s so unfair! At least let me see!”

“Me too! Let me peek!”

It’s not like I didn’t understand how they felt. With my expectations through the roof, I opened the magazine right there in front of the succubi…

A bed of roses was depicted on the first page, and in the middle of it was Vanir’s mask.

Yes. Only Vanir’s mask.

“Aaah! V-V-Vanir’s nude form…”

“T-This is amazing! I can’t believe even the underside of Vanir’s mask is…”

Unlike the excited succubi around me, I could only stare in confusion. Vanir said cheerfully,

“You really are a strange man. To desire my pinup magazine… do you perhaps hold romantic feelings toward me? Sadly, us devils have no gender, so I cannot answer your feelings… Oh, what an intense swell of dark emotions! It’s delicious indeed!”
