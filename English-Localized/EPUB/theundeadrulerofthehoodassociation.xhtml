The Undead Ruler of the Neighborhood Association

From the Digital Edition

Translator: yuNS

Editors: Keel the Swift, Deus Ex Machina

Localization: Sean92us (Utilizing GPT-5.2)

Occurrence: Volume 4

“…Someone brought the real deal here.”

It was almost dusk.

“What is your problem!? Why are you always, always so stingy with me!? All you ever do is make my life harder, you penny-pinching Kazuma!”

Right in the middle of the town graveyard, Aqua and I were arguing.

“U-Um… You two, please… People are going to be here soon, so…”

Wiz, who had been forcefully brought here by Aqua, tried to mediate the situation.

This all began yesterday afternoon.

Among the many requests at the guild was one asking for staff to help run a “test of courage” hosted by the neighborhood association. Aqua, of course, jumped at it, but…

“Are you serious!? It’s just a neighborhood test of courage—don’t bring real undead into it! And what’s the point of a test of courage if nothing’s even scary!?”

“What!? Are you saying Wiz isn’t scary!? Are you saying those stray ghosts over there are scarier!? Apologize! Apologize for hurting a lich’s pride! And Wiz, say something scary, please—make a scary face!”

“A-Aqua, um… I’m actually more hurt by what you just said…”

But what should we do now?

I’m pretty sure the guild had hired some adventurers to play monsters, but at this rate, this idiot was going to ruin the whole thing.

Perhaps noticing my worries-

“Um… It’ll be fine. Leave it to me! Despite how I look, my title of undead ruler isn’t just for show. I’ll make sure to give them a proper scare!”

Wiz declared this with a clenched fist.

Soon the sun set, and the cemetery started to feel like a cemetery. That was when—

“Perfect! No matter how you look at it, that’s a zombie!”

“…You really are talented when it comes to weird side jobs.”

Aqua nodded satisfied, drawing tools in hand.

“I-Is it really that good? What does my face look like right now…? I did say I’d scare everyone, but I’d rather not overdo it…”

Wiz—who Aqua had given Hollywood-level makeup—said that uneasily.

“Well, if nothing else, your zombie makeup is good enough to give an old person a heart attack on sight.”

“I-Is it really!?”

“Oh, they’re coming! First, Kazuma and I will chat with the participants to lower their guard. Then when they least expect it, you jump out and scare them! Wiz, hide!”

“Y-Yes, I understand!”

Once Wiz hid behind a grave, Aqua and I headed over with cheery smiles to greet the participants.

“—Huh? Kazuma took the job too?”

I thought they were just participants, but it turns out that they’re just some familiar adventurers.

Well that makes sense. It’d be pretty lonely if there was just one or two people doing this entire thing.

“Yeah. Aqua and I were going to chat with the participants like this to lower their guard, and then…”

Just as I was explaining-

“B-B-BAHHHHHHH!”

A voice that didn’t quite suit the dark cemetery sounded.

According to plan, Wiz jumped from behind the shadow of the grave.

“Ah, Wiz, it’s a misunderstanding. These guys are here to—”

“Owaaaah!”

Before I could finish explaining, the adventurers screamed in terror.

Ah—wait…

“I-It’s a zombie! The real deal!”

“Damn, that sure gave me a scare, but we’re lucky it showed up before the participants!”

“Ah, shoot, I didn’t bring my weapons.”

All of the adventurers said some version of that.

“…Huh?”

Unable to grasp what was happening, Wiz kept her arms raised like a zombie.

Then, one of the adventurers pointed to Aqua.

“Nah, we don’t need our weapons. We’ve got Aqua here, don’t we?”

“Ah.”

“Wha—? W-What!? P-Please wait…”

Ignoring Wiz-

“Aqua, if you please.”

“W-W-W-Wait! I-I’m not a zombie! Um, I’m not a zombie, so—”

“T-That’s right! She’s not a zombie. It’s just some special makeup…”

As Wiz and I hurriedly tried to resolve the misunderstanding, the adventurers tilted their heads in puzzlement.

“…Oi, this zombie is talking.”

“It even said it’s not a zombie… What’s this? Some kind of rare subspecies?”

“I don’t really get it. I really don’t, but just in case…”

The adventurers nodded their heads.

“Since Turn Undead won’t hurt any living beings, Aqua, please go ahead.”

“…U-Um… I’ll hold back a little, okay?”

—That night, the “zombie” sprinting for its life through the cemetery turned the test of courage into a bustling, lively event.

For some reason, Aqua got a special bonus.
