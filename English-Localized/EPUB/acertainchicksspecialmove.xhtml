A Certain Chick’s Special Move!

Translator: yuNS

Editors: Adam, BlitzBlast, Cannongerbil, Skythewood, Xenthur

Localization: Sean92us (Utilizing GPT-5.2)

Occurrence: Volume 9

“Hey, Kazuma, listen—Emperor Zell finally learned a trick.”

Aqua said, cradling Emperor Zell in her hands.

After our slightly late lunch, everyone slipped into lazy mode.

Sitting across from Darkness, I stared at the chessboard on the table—

“I’ll admit you’re a first-rate performer, but a chick can’t learn tricks. We’re betting next week’s dish duty, so don’t distract me.”

“Hee-hee. I always lose to you, but today I’ve got a huge advantage! You already used your once-a-day Explosion rule against Megumin! I’m winning this—there’s no way I lose!”

I ignored Darkness—eyes glittering with excitement—and refocused on the board.

A little ways off, Megumin—who’d wasted her mana earlier and burned through what little brainpower she had during our match—was sprawled limp on the couch.

If I’d known this was coming, I wouldn’t have gone all-out against Megumin.

“Aqua, Kazuma and Darkness seem busy. How about I be your audience?”

“Oh, are they? Fine, Megumin—I’ll let you be the one to witness Emperor Zell’s magnificent performance.”

Relieved that the annoying girl was gone, I focused on the board again and tried to figure out how to salvage my losing position.

“Well, Kazuma? About time you surrendered, right? When I told my father about my losing streak, he—’the kingdom’s treasured sword,‘ as they call him—taught me this foolproof tactic.”

“For a high-ranking noble, your dad’s pretty childish. He spoils you too much.”

“Ugh, what do I do? Should I tuck the thief in here…?”

“W-What!? I figured a little chick couldn’t do much…!”

“Hmph. You see now, right? He’s not a chick—he’s a dragon. This is nothing for him.”

…

“Hey, Aqua—what did he do? Show me.”

Even though this was a crucial moment in the match, Megumin’s exclamation piqued my curiosity.

“You just said you didn’t want to watch, so why are you asking now? Emperor Zell’s show is over for today. The next one is next month.”

“Hey, that’s not fair—just show me a little. I really want to see it.”

Besides, I wasn’t going to be able to concentrate until I saw it.

“Hey, Kazuma—if you want to see it, just concede. I saw it too. It was amazing. Honestly, it might even beat Aqua’s usual tricks.”

“… It’s that amazing?”

What should I do? When it came to party tricks, I had to admit Aqua was good.

No—she didn’t have many redeeming qualities besides healing magic and party tricks.

“Aqua, please, do it again! I’ve never seen anything that amazing.”

“No, Megumin. That was a performance from the depths of Emperor Zell’s soul—his way of repaying my careful daily training. It’s not something you can just do on command.”

Aqua and Megumin ignored me—who hadn’t seen a thing—and got all excited on their own.

“I couldn’t help being impressed after seeing that performance. Kazuma, are you really going to be the only one who hasn’t seen it?”

Darkness taunted me while I wavered.

“You’ll regret it if you don’t. Emperor Zell’s lifespan might shorten if he does it too many times. He’s asleep now because it used up all his energy, right?”

“Right? If Emperor Zell doesn’t want to, I can’t do anything about it… Ah!? Emperor Zell woke up! Are you okay!? You’re doing it!? You want to perform again!?”

“Ah! Emperor Zell just…! Kazuma, Darkness! Emperor Zell is ready!”

What did “ready” even mean? It bothered me a lot.

And Aqua said if I missed this chance, the next one would be next month.

“Hey, Aqua! Quit messing around and get over here—I want to see. Darkness, time-out.”

“No. If you leave your seat, you forfeit. How can you walk away before the match is decided? Choose! The trick, or the match!”

Ugh…

“All right, Megumin! Watch closely and witness Emperor Zell’s special performance!”

“If I get to see it again, even I might fall for Emperor Zell!”

Damn it!

“Fine, I concede! Aqua, wait—I want to see too!”

The moment I conceded to Darkness, Aqua and Megumin high-fived.

“…?”

“Then watch carefully.”

Darkness, wearing a proud grin, started putting away the chess pieces. That gave me a bad feeling.

“Now, behold Emperor Zell’s special trick!”

Aqua cradled Emperor Zell with a dopey smile, then cupped her hands around him…

After a moment, Emperor Zell curled into a ball, shut his eyes, and fell asleep.

“He falls asleep when I do this. Early to bed and early to rise makes a chick healthy, wealthy, and wise, right?”

Early to eat and early to poop makes him wealthy?

And anyway, cupping your hands over a chick until it falls asleep isn’t a “performance”—it’s just what chicks do.

… Wait a second.

Megumin had challenged me out of nowhere earlier today, and Aqua had lured me in with Emperor Zell’s so-called performance. Was this all Darkness’s…?

“Here, Aqua. Megumin. As agreed. But Father really is despicable… Using something off the chessboard to force the opponent to concede…”

“Hey! Wait a minute!!”
