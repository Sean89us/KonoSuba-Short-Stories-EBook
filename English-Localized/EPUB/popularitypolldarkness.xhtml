[This is the third short story released on the Sneaker Bunko site for the top three winners of the popularity poll.]

That day.

When I came to, the first thing I saw was Darkness creeping into my room like she was about to stage a night raid.

“… This is my first time, so I’d really appreciate it if you’d pay attention to the mood when you do stuff like this.”

“I-Idiot, what are you saying!? Don’t say such misleading things the first thing in the morning! What were you going to do if someone saw us and got the wrong impression!?”

To be specific, it wasn’t actually night, so calling it a night raid wasn’t exactly accurate.

Darkness quickly hastened to shut me up, but it’s already too late…

“A-Awawawawawa! Darkness is leaning over Kazuma first thing in the morning!”

“Aqua!? Wait, that’s not it! I have something I need to talk to Kazuma about and wanted to wake him…!”

Aqua said, peeking through the crack in the door, and Darkness hastily shouted back while covering me with my blanket.

“—So in other words: you tripped over me while waiting for a good moment to wake me up, and I woke up right as you were trying to get off me without waking me?”

“Y-Yes, that’s exactly it! I thought it was uncharacteristically clumsy of me too…!”

Darkness seemed to be on the verge of tears as I finally understood what happened after much explanation.

“So that’s when you thought, ’Since Kazuma’s already awake…‘ and decided to go through with it, right?”

“Aqua, don’t butt in! Who would do something that stupid!? Ah, dammit…!”

After enduring our constant teasing, Darkness took a moment to compose herself and said with a newly serious expression.

“Kazuma, please help me! Chris has been acting really strange lately!”

—While watching over her from the shadows, Darkness whispered to Aqua and me.

“… Chris has been hanging out with some unsavory characters around here lately. I wanted to ask her what she was up to myself, but, um…”

Darkness trailed off, glancing between Aqua and me.

“But… what? I won’t get it if you don’t say it.”

When I urged her to continue, Darkness reluctantly turned towards me.

“… Well, thinking about it, I also hang out with some unsavory characters…”

“Oh? Oh? Darkness, you sure said something interesting, didn’t you!”

“Kazuma, hold her down! I’m going to tie her hair into pigtails and parade her around the adventurers’ guild!”

As the two unsavory characters closed in on Darkness, she motioned for us to be quiet between bouts of nearly silent pleading.

“So this is why you brought me along… Chris has the Enemy Detection skill, so she’ll easily figure out if anyone else is following her.”

“Yes, exactly. With you here, we can tail her much more closely with your Lurk skill. Normally there’s no way I could’ve followed her from this close… Ah, that’s him, Kazuma! Anyone would say that man looks to be up to no good… Ah!? He brought out a suspicious bag!”

Following Darkness’s pointing, I saw Chris talking to a man wearing a thick leather jacket.

The muscle-bound man with deep scars on his cheeks had a bag filled with white powder in his hands—

“That’s the guy who runs the cake shop. He apparently used to be an adventurer, but he always dreamed of opening a cake shop. Looks like he’s giving Chris good flour and sugar at her request.”

“Hey, Darkness, get a hold of yourself! Shouldn’t you be happy we were worried over nothing!?”

Darkness fell limp for a moment upon hearing Aqua’s words, but quickly leapt to her feet.

“Y-Yes, that man is just the start! There’s another man I’m actually worried about!”

With a slight blush, Darkness declared to cover up her misunderstanding.

“—Look into that alley. You’ll understand why I’m concerned at a glance.”

Chris’s next destination was a shadowy alley.

Following Darkness’s instructions, I saw a man standing in the alley in a white coat with a meat cleaver hanging off his hip.

His white coat seemed stained with something, so I used Farsight to take a closer look, and those stains turned out to be blood…!

“That’s the butcher. He specializes in chickens and other birds… Ah, Chris just got a bag of eggs from him. That’s a relief, Darkness. Looks like she’s just out shopping.”

“Darkness, calm down. Instead of beating yourself up, just be glad nothing happened to Chris.”

Darkness buried her face in her hands and seemed to grow smaller.

—Just then,

“Say, you guys have been following me since morning. What’s going on?”

She must have closed the distance while Aqua and I were busy comforting Darkness.

“—Ahahaha! How foolish of you, Darkness! I’m a devout follower of Eris who loves justice, you know? There’s no way I’d end up with bad people!”

After hearing our explanation, Chris let out a hearty laugh.

“Kuh, I have nothing to say… To think that I would doubt a friend like that…”

Chris let out a chuckle towards Darkness who fell onto her knees.

“Heheheh, I’m actually happy about that. After all, it just means you care about me that much, right, Darkness?”

“Uggh…. That might be true, but I judged others by their appearances and even cast my doubts on them. As a servant of Eris, I don’t think I can forgive myself…”

Darkness seems to place a lot of importance on the weirdest things sometimes.

Chris placed a hand on Darkness’s shoulder.

“It’s fine. You might have gone a little overboard, but it was all out of concern for a friend. I’m sure Lady Eris will forgive you.”

As Chris said that with a smile, Aqua interjected with something completely unnecessary.

“Do you really think so? She’s really serious and inflexible, so she might not actually forgive Darkness.”

“Hey, Aqua, you’re just making Darkness more depressed, so cut it out!”

Darkness buried her head between her knees and shut herself out from the world.

Though, there was something that bothered me.

“Say, Chris, you bought quite a lot of things today. What are you planning to make with that?”

“Eh!? That’s, um, well…”

Chris stuttered while trying and failing not to look at Darkness.

So it has something to do with Darkness, then?

“It’s a cake! Flour, sugar, and eggs are definitely for a cake! Chris, is someone’s birthday coming up?”

At Aqua’s sudden words, Darkness and Chris both seemed to stiffen up in shock.

… Ah, I see what’s going on.

“Alright, Aqua, let’s go home. I’ll give you some money, so buy some good wine for later, okay?”

Having her plans for a surprise party revealed, Chris stood with trembling shoulders and a red face.

Darkness, too, was trembling in much the same way.

I turned towards the two of them and said,

“I’ll have Megumin cook up a lavish feast, so you two come home early, okay?”

On the way home, I bought a plush for a certain someone who, despite usually being serious and stiff, has a soft spot for cute, fancy things.
