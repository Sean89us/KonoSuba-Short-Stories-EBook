The One with Azure Lightning Behind Her

Translator: Cannongerbil

Editors: Keel The Swift, Ulti

Localization: Sean92us (Utilizing GPT-5.2)

Occurrence: After Volume 14

While I was wandering around the Crimson Demon Village, I found Aqua shadowboxing.

“…What are you doing?”

“Oh, Kazuma, what a coincidence. I ran into this gorgeous lady a little while ago, and she said, ‘Since you’re friends with Megumin, allow me to offer you a warning. Wind… Darkness… and a divine light… please remember these words.’ Then she disappeared right in front of me. I don’t get what any of it means, so I’ve been standing guard to make sure she can’t escape.”

That’s the same trick the Crimson Demons showed us last time: refraction magic that makes it look like they teleported.

“Sacred Break Spell!”

“!?”

As Aqua shouted the spell, I heard someone gasp and scramble out of the way.

“See? You heard that, right? That little yelp when I pretended to cast something?”

“I-I kind of feel bad for her, so cut it out…”

Just then—

“I look away from you two for one minute and you’re causing trouble again…”

“Uh, try not to bully my people too much.”

Darkness and Megumin had walked up behind us.

“Hey, Darkness, there’s a pretty lady hiding around there, so go find her and catch her for me. I want to ask what she meant.”

“Don’t worry, I’ll do it for you—”

Darkness grabbed my collar before I could move.

“Darkness, keep a tight hold on that man. Aqua, it’s a common tradition here to tell tourists some mysterious phrase and then vanish. There’s no deeper meaning, so let her go.”

What an annoying tradition.

“By the way, what are you two doing? Out for a walk?”

“No, we’re… Actually, it’ll be faster to just show you.”

Megumin pointed into the distance.

“Yo, the one with azure lightning behind her! Morning. Back from Axel?”

“Roaring Thunder Yunyun! Morning! How’s the outside world? Anything changed?”

“A-Ah, good morning! Good morning! It doesn’t seem like much has changed!”

Yunyun stood there, getting bombarded with greetings by every passing Crimson Demon.

Probably from being called those titles, Yunyun’s face was beet-red, and tears clung to the corners of her eyes.

“Kazuma, what do you think after seeing that?”

“It looks like they’re bullying her.”

Megumin frowned at my honest answer.

“No, seriously. Look at Yunyun —she’s got everyone crowding around her.”

I took another look…

“Oh, Yunyun, that azure lightning behind you is crackling brightly today too!”

“Roaring Thunder Yunyun, did you put on a little weight? You look… a bit more filled out…”

“Please stop calling me that! And I didn’t get fat —I just grew up!”

…

“So? What do you think after seeing her stand there with that smug little frown while everyone fawns over her and calls her by her title!?”

“I-Is that so? She doesn’t look like she’s enjoying it…”

Apparently at her limit, Megumin marched over to Yunyun in the middle of the crowd.

“Yunyun, you look awfully pleased having everyone call you such a cool title.”

“What are you talking about!? Do I look happy right now!? Are your eyes made of glass!?”

“They’re the same red ones you’ve got!”

As if a rabid dog had just appeared, the Crimson Demons who’d surrounded Yunyun scattered in a hurry.

“…Ah, I see. Megumin really can’t be honest. For all her complaining, she still went over to help Yunyun when she’s suffering under those embarrassing nicknames…”

Darkness nodded at the sight of them and smiled warmly…

“Megumin, you had a title back in Axel too!”

“I couldn’t care less about some embarrassing title like that! Hey, ‘Azure Lightning,’ trade nicknames with me!”

…

“Do you really believe that?”

“…Maybe not…”

Far from Axel—

“I don’t like my current title, but yours is…”

“Fine, I’ll challenge you! And when I win, I’m taking your title for myself!”

A very familiar scene was playing out—

“Sorry, I don’t want your title, so I’m going to pass…”

“W-What!? You’re the one who never stops badgering me to accept your challenges!”
