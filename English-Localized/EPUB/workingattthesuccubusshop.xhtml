Dust Spinoff 5 Short Story 1: Working at the Succubus Shop

Translator: Cannongerbil

Localization: Sean92us (Utilizing GPT-5.2)

Occurrence: Dust Spinoff Volume 5

“Thanks for having me today.”

“Dust, do you understand that money disappears when you spend it?”

I’d tried to greet her cheerfully, but the Loli Succubus hit me with a question that stupid.

Normally I’d fire right back, but I couldn’t afford that today—literally.

“Don’t say that. If you just sit on money, the economy doesn’t move, you know? My money makes the rounds in town and keeps the wheels of prosperity turning.”

“Your pocket change doesn’t even come close to what the lowest-ranking minor noble has, you know?”

“You really aren’t cute at all…”

“I don’t really want you to find me cute anyway. But Vanir is a different story.”

She must’ve been imagining Vanir praising her, because a blush crept up her face as she fidgeted excitedly.

This was a succubus shop, so she was wearing lingerie-like clothing. Normally, bouncing around in something that revealing would be dangerous in all sorts of ways, but with her petite build, it did absolutely nothing for me.

All the other succubi had amazing figures, so why was she the only one who didn’t seem to develop at all?

“Well, everyone values different things.”

“The way you look at me when you say that kind of pisses me off.”

“Hey, you shouldn’t use that tone with a regular customer. Sorry about that, Dust.”

The succubus in charge apologized on her behalf.

She was the Loli Succubus’s superior in every way, and seeing her… assets shift when she bowed made me forgive absolutely everything.

“Don’t worry about it. More importantly, I’m in your care for today’s work.”

“Yes. We’ll be counting on you as a handyman and bouncer.”

The succubus in charge bowed, Loli Succubus reluctantly followed.

Due to certain events, I was barred from working at the Adventurer’s Guild for several days, so I had to make do with a part time job here for now.

“All right, then. I’ll leave the details to you.”

“Understood… Dust, please do your best not to do anything unnecessary.”

“Yeah. I won’t do anything, so don’t worry.”

“Um, please actually do some work…”

I’d been assigned to the Loli Succubus for the day, so I figured I’d try not to cause any incidents.

She brought me by the doors and told me to please stand guard there, so I leaned against the wall and looked out into the store.

Just seeing succubi walk around in outfits like that made me want to earn money as fast as humanly possible.

It’s still early, so there aren’t many customers yet, but soon enough this place will be filled with numerous burly adventurers.

Almost on cue, a pair of adventurers I’d run into now and then strolled into the store.

“What kind of dream are you getting today? Being lectured by an older woman is great, but getting pampered by someone younger sounds nice too.”

“I’m going for a romance with someone way above my social status. It’s a dream, so I might as well pick something impossible in real life.”

“Welcome, valued customers.”

I was an employee today, so I welcomed them like I’d been told. They just stared at my face instead.

“What are you doing here, Dust? This is kind of creepy.”

“If you’re ordering a dream, then go home and sleep already. What are you doing lurking by the door?”

It seems like they didn’t even consider that I’m working here. But, well, I suppose that’s to be expected.

“I’m working here today. If you’re looking for a dream, I’ve got a few suggestions you might appreciate.”

“Don’t need it! Nobody wants to hear about your tastes!”

“What!? I’m genuinely offering to help, and this is how you treat me!?”

Just as I considered teaching this cheeky bastard a lesson, someone whacked me on the head.

“What are you doing!?”

I turned around, and standing right there was Loli Succubus with a serving tray in hand.

“Please don’t pick fights with the customers! I apologize for any trouble this good-for-nothing has caused.”

“Ahh, it’s fine. Lolisa, you must have your hands full dealing with this guy every day.”

“You two have been spending a lot of time together recently. Don’t tell me you two are…”

The two of them glanced between the two of us before looking at each other.

“Wait, that is not—”

“That’s impossible! There’s no way I’d be with a debt-ridden, scummy, worthless guy like him! Besides, I’m a succubus! The only one I have eyes for is Vanir!”

T-This girl. There’s no need to deny it so strongly that you’re red in the face.

“You got it. I have no interest in you either.”

“The way you’re saying it like you don’t even see me as a woman kind of pisses me off.”

“You really are a troublesome girl, you know that?”

While we were arguing, the two adventurers went to take a seat and started filling out their forms.

Loli Succubus seemed to have calmed down after saying what she wanted to say, and went back to her work.

I had nothing else to do, so I returned to my job of standing at the door.

“Good work today. I’ll be back tomorrow.”

After work ended and I got my pay, I said that.

“Please don’t come back anymore!” x3

But they instantly rejected me.

“Dust, you keep peeking at the forms customers fill out and making comments like ‘Ooh, you’ve got good taste,’ and ‘Isn’t that level of play a little too advanced?,’ so you’re scaring customers away!”
