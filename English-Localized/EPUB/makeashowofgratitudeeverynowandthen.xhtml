“Oh! It might be summer, but that outfit is still way too sexy. Are you trying to seduce me?”

The first thing I said after getting back to the mansion made Darkness—who was walking around in a skimpy outfit—freeze in place.

“H-Hey, what are you saying? The moment you get back, you say something like that…! It’s too hot, so it can’t be helped—I’m not trying to seduce you!”

“You say that, but you’re a little excited about it, aren’t you?”

“What? What’s wrong with you today?”

Megumin, who was playing a board game with Aqua, gaped at our exchange.

“What is this, all of a sudden? Kazuma might harass people a lot, but that’s way too direct.”

I glanced at Megumin.

“Megumin’s in a one-piece today, huh? With how tomboyish she is, she should just commit and go naked.”

“What’s wrong with you!? Why are you suddenly saying stuff like that?”

Megumin panicked, while Aqua sat across from her with her arms crossed and nodded along.

No, actually…

“A magic item that reveals your thoughts?” they said in unison.

That’s right. Wiz asked me to test out this new magic item. When Wiz—wearing a skimpy summer dress—looked up at me with those pleading eyes, I happily agreed.

So yeah.

Right now, I was wearing the hat Wiz had handed me.

“I-I wasn’t asking why you agreed… No, wait, that was your thoughts leaking out. But why would Wiz make something like that…?”

“She developed it to make it possible to communicate with monsters and animals. Apparently she wanted to test it on livestock—she mentioned taking it to a rancher she knows.”

“Why didn’t you stop her? Wouldn’t that make things difficult for the rancher?”

I looked at Aqua, who hadn’t moved at all and was completely focused on the board in front of her.

I took off the hat and plopped it onto her head.

“What should I do? If I move this piece here, Megumin, with her twisted personality, will definitely target it. What do I do? Betting a side dish on one match was a mistake. If this keeps up, I’ll be stuck eating plain rice with just condiments tonight. I should’ve settled it with rock-paper-scissors. All I’d have to do is secretly cast blessing magic to win.”

The moment Aqua’s voice started coming from the top of her head, Megumin pounced on her.

“Hey, what are you doing, Megumin!? The board’s a mess now! That match doesn’t count, right? We don’t have time for another game, so let’s settle this with rock-paper-scissors!!”

“That startled me, but this is my chance! Did the heat get to her? It’s great that Megumin is as impatient as Darkness!”

“So that’s what you think of me!? I’m an Archwizard known for being cool and collected—I’m nothing like Darkness!!”

“Wait, I’m not that impatient…! And what do you two usually think about me?”

I gently lifted the hat off Aqua and put it back on myself.

“That’s how the magic item works… Oh, the big-boobs girl is still staring at me suspiciously. Fine—then you wear it.”

“B-Big-boobs girl!? Is that what you call me in your head!? S-Stop! I don’t want to wear that thing—wear it yourself if you want!”

“Tch. I was going to ask how many times a day you masturbate once you put it on.”

“A-An aristocratic lady wouldn’t do something as shameless as that! Ah, stop! Really, I’m not lying! I’m not lying, so don’t force the hat on me!!”

Seeing Darkness’s reaction, Megumin sighed.

“Honestly… it’d be better if this magic item never gets developed. Kazuma, take it off. Just tell Wiz what I said.”

“Fine, fine. Whatever. I got showered with icy glares from women on the way home, too. I’m not going anywhere near that place for a while.”

“What were you thinking the whole way here? Were there any police patrolling?”

I took off the hat, stretched lazily, and walked to the kitchen.

“By the way, Aqua, that rock-paper-scissors just now doesn’t count. You actually cast blessing in secret…? Don’t get so bold with your cheating. Let’s play a proper match!”

“H-How do you know!? No, you’re bluffing. Where’s your proof? Go on, show me proof that I secretly used magic!”

“Give it up, Aqua. Tonight you’re eating plain rice with condiments.”

“Why-?”

Listening to the commotion in the living room, I set the hat on top of a wine bottle on the table and started making dinner…

“Thank you for tending my grave and telling me stories about your adventures all this time.”

The sudden voice made me turn toward the hat in surprise.

“What was that…? My imagination? No, I heard it pretty clearly…”

Just then, Aqua’s wailing came from the living room.

“Kazuma, Kazuma—! Those two are so mean! Hey, make extra dinner tonight!”

…They were exhausting.

I made an extra dish—
