Dust Spinoff Short Story 2: Observing Megumin

Translator: Cannongerbil

Editors: Ulti, Xenthur

Localization: Sean92us (Utilizing GPT-5.2)

Occurrence: After Dust Spinoff Volume 1

While I was at the adventurers’ guild, looking for easy, profitable quests like I always do, I got a strange, “off the record” request from Kazuma. It seems the guild views him as the caretaker of his party, so they keep making him clean up after their messes.

He wanted me to investigate what his party gets up to when he’s not around.

He’s a close friend I owe a lot to, and he even promised a decent reward, so I had no reason to refuse.

In that case… today, I’ll observe that explosion maniac.

She’s a Crimson Demon like Yunyun. According to Vanir, the Crimson Demons are a belligerent clan whose members are born with huge mana reserves.

You can tell they have a unique sense of aesthetics just from their naming alone. And in that sense, Megumin is considered a prodigy.

The reason: aside from Explosion, she has no intention of learning any other spell.

Normally, she drags Kazuma outside the town to cast Explosion every day, but after he started refusing because of the cold, she began making the trip with other people.

…You can only call yourself a true resident of Axel after you get used to hearing an explosion every day.

“Did I make you wait?”

“I think it’s about time you stopped doing this.”

Today’s companion is Yunyun.

“That’ll never happen. Don’t you know I’ll die if I don’t cast an explosion every day?”

“I’ve never heard of such a thing! …Still, whenever I carry you back lately, the townspeople look at me kindly. Someone even told me, ’Thanks for your hard work,‘ the other day. It made me a little happy…”

Yunyun might grumble, but even through her frown, she looked genuinely happy to be relied on.

“Stop complaining. Let’s go.”

“Wha—why are you ordering me around? I’m the one accompanying you! Hey, did you hear me? W-Wait up!”

Despite her protests, Yunyun still ended up following Megumin out of town.

At this point, it was less that she was accompanying her and more that she couldn’t refuse.

It always feels like if you knelt and begged, she’d agree to just about anything.

Trailing behind them, I followed the two to a stretch of barren land.

…More accurately, it only became barren because of that explosion girl.

I think this used to be a small forest. Now the soil was exposed, and the area was dotted with craters.

“This place is full of holes! You need to fill them in properly later, or you’ll cause people a lot of trouble.”

“I’ll leave that to you. I’m a very busy person.”

While Yunyun hurried to fill the craters in with magic, Megumin casually fired off her Explosion and carved a brand-new crater into the landscape.

Yunyun hoisted the collapsed Megumin onto her back with practiced ease and complained the whole way back.

By the time they reached town, Megumin had recovered enough to walk on her own and climbed down from Yunyun’s back.

“I’ve fulfilled your request, so—just like we agreed—let’s settle our duel right here!”

“Even after losing so many times… You really don’t learn, do you? So what are we competing in today?”

“Ah, um… We should settle this with magic… but Megumin only knows Explosion, and she already used it today, so that won’t work.”

“I’ve already used my magic for today. I don’t mean to brag, but I’m useless for the rest of the day.”

“That’s not something to brag about!”

“Aah! What did you say? Say that again!”

“D-Don’t get angry.”

“You’ve always been bad at reading the room, Yunyun, but hasn’t it gotten worse lately? It must be the bad influence of that friend you recently made.”

Yunyun clutched Megumin’s clothes and tearfully denied it. Who was this friend they were talking about?

Megumin is Yunyun’s childhood friend, so I could rule her out. Other than that, the only people Yunyun had been interacting with lately were Vanir and that beautiful shop owner.

Did she make a new friend without me knowing? Seeing how desperately she denied it, it must be a pretty nasty character.

In the end, they settled on an eating contest, and Megumin won by a landslide.

It’s hard to believe she can eat that much with such a petite frame. Still, she doesn’t seem to have grown at all.

Afterward, she chatted with the Axis Cult priest and Chris about something or other, but it didn’t seem like she caused trouble for anyone today.

She even used her Explosion somewhere it wouldn’t cause trouble for the town. The only thing she troubled was a patch of barren ground.

Yunyun said doing that would cause trouble for others, but she didn’t sound like she meant it, so that doesn’t count.

After a long day, Megumin finally went home.

Yunyun looked genuinely lonely as she watched Megumin enter the mansion.

Bah, screw it. I guess I’ll treat her to dinner.
