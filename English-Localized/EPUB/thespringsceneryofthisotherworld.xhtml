Sneaker Bunko 30th anniversary event short story: The Spring Scenery of this Other World

[TL Note: This short story was given away to attendees at the special anniversary event that Sneaker bunko held earlier in the spring. This is not the short story that showed up in the special anniversary edition Sneaker bunko released]

Just after noon, warm sunlight streamed gently through the window.

I was enjoying a short post-lunch nap by the window when a sudden loud noise obliterated the cozy atmosphere.

“Kazuma! Kazuma! Let’s go see the blossoms! The cherry trees are gorgeous today!”

Aqua being that cheerful gave me a bad feeling, so I kept pretending to sleep.

“…Are you sleeping? Hey, Kazuma, you’re awake, aren’t you?”

Just as Aqua started shaking me,

“Aqua, it’s too cruel to force a sleeping man awake. Let’s just enjoy the flower viewing with the three of us.”

“True… Kazuma must be worn out from all his adventures. Let’s enjoy the flowers without him.”

Megumin and Darkness said, and Aqua reluctantly backed off.

“It would’ve been more fun with more people, but… oh well. We even managed to get some high-quality snacks to go with the wine.”

…High-quality snacks.

No, no, no. Don’t fall for it. Nothing good ever comes from getting involved with plants in this world.

Even if this world had cherry blossoms, they’d be the kind that spit sap in your eyes and slice you to ribbons with razor vines.

Then Aqua would cry, Darkness would clutch her head, Megumin would blow up the whole area, and I’d have to clean up after them.

I shoved any thoughts of high-quality snacks out of my mind and stayed perfectly still…

“After all, the cherry blossom spirits always show up completely naked. It’d be too much excitement for Kazuma, so…”

“Hey, what are you three doing!? Hurry up and grab your stuff! You didn’t forget the wine, did you? Alright, let’s go!”

When I bolted upright and started barking orders, the three of them stared at me like I was walking garbage—

—Cherry blossoms.

“Waaaaah! Kazuma! Kazuma! Please save me!”

The flower that heralded spring. The one every Japanese person loved.

“Kuzuma, quick, pour the wine on the ground! The cherry blossom spirits will go back into the earth to drink once you do!”

—Cherry blossoms.

“A Crimson Demon like me isn’t going to lose to some stupid plant! Buy me time! I’ll blow them all away with Explosion!!”

A flower that only bloomed for a short time—and that’s exactly why people adored it.

“Don’t look down on us Japanese! Even if I die, I’m getting a good look at you cherry blossom spirits!”

I stopped Darkness just as she started to pour the wine.

“Come on! Face me!!!”

With a yell, I charged straight into the cloud of petals—

In the end, Aqua cried, Darkness clutched her head, Megumin used Explosion and blew up the entire area, and I had to pay for one destroyed cherry tree.
