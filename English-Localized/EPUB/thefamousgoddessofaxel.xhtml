The Famous Goddess of Axel

Translator: Cannongerbil

Editors: Striker, Ulti

Localization: Sean92us (Utilizing GPT-5.2)

Occurrence: After Volume 16

I was walking through Axel to pick up dinner when an older woman I didn’t recognize stopped to flirt with me.

“Excuse me—may I have a moment?”

She was clutching a small bottle and looked genuinely worried.

“You’re one of Aqua’s companions, right? I want to buy Aqua’s delicious water, but… is she not selling today?”

“Sorry, I’m not following.”

So no, she wasn’t flirting with me. Shame.

“No, usually Aqua brings her sparkling, delicious water out around this time, but…”

She was doing that?

“Well, something came up. She isn’t in town right now.”

“When I cook with Aqua’s water, my children don’t have allergic reactions…”

She slumped and started to walk away.

It was rare to see Aqua be genuinely useful to anyone.

And just as I was briefly impressed, a booming voice came from the main street.

“Gather round! Gather round! I am Pierre—one of the great street performers competing for the number-one spot in the capital! Prepare to be amazed!”

When I looked over, a surprisingly big crowd had already gathered.

“Axel is famous for making street performers run off in tears! Will my acts satisfy your discerning eyes!?”

The great street performer Pierre gave an exaggerated bow.

This was the first I’d heard about Axel having a reputation for traumatizing entertainers…

Scattered applause met him, and he reached into his bag.

“First, a greeting! What could be in this bag, I wonder…!”

A few kids in the crowd started shouting excitedly.

“A rookie killer! A rookie killer bigger than the bag will jump out!”

“No, a flock of crows! They’ll fly out and draw a picture in the sky! Aqua did it!”

Pierre froze after hearing what his audience apparently considered “normal.”

“…No. This is just candy I’m handing out as a greeting. I haven’t started my act yet. This is just a candy box. Here, young lady—have a sweet.”

He hurriedly passed out candy, trying to reset the tone.

“It would seem the people of this town are no strangers to street performances! Very well! I shall skip straight to my eighteenth act! Behold—the disappearing act!”

At Pierre’s words, a woman in a bunny-girl outfit strode up beside him.

“Disappearing act? Don’t tell me…”

“He’s joking, right? There’s no way he’s being serious, right?”

Hearing the crowd’s astonished reaction, Pierre sounded delighted.

“Oh? That got your attention! Behold! When I cover her with this cloak, she will vanish in an instant! Of course, this is an ordinary cloak! No tricks, no magic!”

Screams erupted.

“Stop! What are you trying to do to that lady!?”

“This guy’s insane! Somebody call the police—call an adventurer—call anyone!”

With kids starting to cry and adults sprinting off for help, Pierre finally realized something was very wrong with the vibe here.

“I-I don’t know why everyone’s so worked up, but… maybe watch before you judge!? All right! Is everyone ready!? One, two, three!”

The moment Pierre covered the woman with the cloak, the crowd gasped.

At the same time, the cloak floated gently to the ground…

“N-No! Th-The lady—!”

“She disappeared! This guy’s dangerous!”

“Get someone here! He’s a criminal! A murderer!”

Watching the panic spiral out of control, Pierre’s mask of composure finally cracked.

“Wait! This is just a show! Why is this happening!? What is wrong with these people!? Everyone’s reactions are completely unhinged!”

After the policemen who rushed over heard the crowd’s explanation…

“Great street performer Pierre, please come with us to the station.”

“Why!? What is wrong with this town!? I performed an act in the street! Why am I being arrested!?”

“Because you made that woman vanish! You said there were no tricks or magic!”

“There’s obviously a trick! That woman can cast Teleport—!”

As Pierre got surrounded by the police, I finally understood why the people of Axel were reacting like this.

It was because the party-trick goddess in my party really could make things disappear with no tricks and no magic.

“So it was a trick after all. Once you’ve seen the real thing…”

“It was just Teleport? What a boring trick…”

“Come to think of it, I don’t see Aqua anywhere. She usually shows up way before now to crush the performer’s spirit.”

Selling water. Ruining street acts. Doing party tricks.

I thought I knew Aqua pretty well, but apparently there was still a lot I didn’t know.

With that on my mind, I finished shopping and walked back to my mansion…

“Ah, Kazuma, good timing! Aqua ran up a huge tab at the bar! They just sent the receipt…”

When I got her back, I was going to have to keep a much closer eye on her.
